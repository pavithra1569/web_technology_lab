Our Story

Established in 2025, ABI Art Gallery is a haven for contemporary and modern art, featuring both emerging and established artists. Our mission is to provide a vibrant platform for artistic expression, cultural exchange, and to cultivate a deeper appreciation for the arts within the community.

Our Vision

At ABI Art Gallery, we believe that art is a powerful medium that transcends boundaries and speaks to the soul. Inspired by the legacies of DAG, Vadehra Art Gallery, and Tulika Arts, we strive to foster creativity and innovation while preserving the rich traditions of fine arts.

Artist Collaborations

We take pride in collaborating with talented artists from across the globe, offering them a space to experiment, engage, and connect with art enthusiasts. Our curated exhibitions span painting, sculpture, mixed media, digital art, and immersive installations.

Past Exhibitions & Events

Over the years, we have hosted several acclaimed exhibitions, including:

  • Echoes of the Soul – A journey through contemporary surrealism.
  • Abstract Narratives – Exploring the language of colors and forms.
  • Timeless Traditions – A celebration of Indian heritage through modern interpretations.
  • Fusion of Forms – Where traditional techniques meet digital innovation.

Engaging the Community

ABI Art Gallery is not just an exhibition space—it is a community. We regularly organize art workshops, guided tours, panel discussions, and interactive sessions to engage art lovers of all ages. Our educational programs inspire young artists and art enthusiasts to explore their creative potential.

Join us as we continue to push the boundaries of artistic expression and make art accessible to all.
